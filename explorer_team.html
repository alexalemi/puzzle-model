Team Model Explorer

Joint solo+team Bayesian model for jigsaw puzzle solving times

Alexander A. Alemi Main Explorer
About this page

This explorer presents a joint solo+team Bayesian model for jigsaw puzzle solving times. It extends the solo-only Model 2r with team mechanics: when multiple puzzlers work together, their individual rates combine via logsumexp (parallel rate addition), subject to Amdahl's law (serial fraction) and per-bucket residual corrections.

For solo solves (K=1), the team model reduces exactly to Model 2r — logsumexp of a single alpha is just that alpha, and all team corrections vanish. This means solo predictions are unchanged while team predictions benefit from the additional structure.

The model is fitted jointly on solo, duo, and group data from speedpuzzling.com, myspeedpuzzling.com, USA Jigsaw, and a personal dataset. Team members who also have solo data provide the key link — their individual ability estimates are informed by both solo and team observations.

Ratings are in milliBels (mB): 1000 mB = factor of 10 in time, 100 mB ≈ 26% change. The Prediction Calculator supports team predictions — add multiple team members and see the combined prediction with Amdahl and eta corrections applied.

How to use this page: Data Overview summarizes the combined dataset. Model Specification describes the team formula. Model Comparison compares solo-only vs joint on the same test set. Team Deep Dive shows team-specific parameters (serial fraction, eta corrections). Model Deep Dive covers shared parameters. Prediction Calculator supports both solo and team predictions. Rankings shows puzzler and puzzle tables from the joint model.
